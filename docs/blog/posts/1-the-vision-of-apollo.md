## What is Apollo?

Apollo is a fresh take on modding Mario Kart Wii. "But why?" you may ask. As outlined in this [open letter](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1xKx21TaXAYN8lYOTMn_rIX2z6LgCiiaBfMuJnCj7vzk/edit "Show me!"), there are a number of issues in the state of popular Mario Kart Wii modding.

!!! info
    This is not a dig at or me trying to cause a stir with any members/developers in the MKW community, rather providing an alternative for users who agree with the open letter.

## When will we start to see an alpha/beta release?

I'm not too sure yet. The logistics of creating the right system, toolchain, etc. is still completely undecided, so stay tuned for a future blogpost with more details on that front.
